Benefits of Playing a Musical Instrument

Problems look mighty small from 150 miles up

Posted by My Personal Blog on August 24, 2014

The Chinese Philosopher Confucius said long aho that "Music produces a kind of pleasure whice human nature cannot do without."Playing musiocal insturment has many benifits and can bring joy to you and to everyonearound you.

Increases the capacity of your memory

Research has shown taht both listening to music and playing a musical instrument stimulate your brain and can increase your memory.

Is guitar music on the way out?.

Classic guitar bands will always be listened to just as we still listen Bach and Beethoven today, but when it comes to future guitar music it's only the fat cats of the music industry that can decide. The trouble is everybody has got lazy. No one can be bothered to learn and master an instrument. It's much easier just to sample someone else playing from yesteryear and produce some processed beat from a computer. It's laziness from the industry too, It's much easier to look up people on YouTube whilst eating breakfast than go to a dingy venue at some ungodly hour.

Being a guitarist in a hard working rock band myself, it is more and more frustrating with every season of the X-factor how we can get the world out of this dark time for music. It's time for a revolution, there are still plenty of hard working, talented rock bands out there waiting to be discovered. They just need someone with Jacobs big enough to take a chance and sign them. Long live Rock.
